1. GENERAL REQUIREMENTS

All Applicants shall satisfy these requirements in addition to the specific requirement for a specific category. An Applicant must:
a. be a Ghanaian by birth;
b. be of good character;
c. be without criminal records;
d. not have been dismissed from any public service or any other employment;
e. not be more than the age stated for a specific category by 1/11/2025;
f. be at least 5 feet 8 inches (173cm) tall for males and 5 feet 4 inches (163cm) for females;
g. be physically and medically fit by Ghana Police Service standards;
h. have 5 passes in BECE including English and Mathematics;
i. be ready to undergo basic Police training and other forms of training;
j. have completed National Service for graduate applicants.

2. CATEGORIES OF ENTRY
A. GENERAL DUTY RECRUITS

In addition to the general requirements an applicant:
a. must not be less than 18 years of age and not more than 30 years by 1/11/2025.
b. must possess a minimum of five passes in SSSCE/WASSCE including Mathematics and English.

B. TRADESMEN RECRUITS

In addition to the general requirements, an applicant who applies to this category must satisfy the requirements stated under the specific sub-category:

i. MOTOR VEHICLE MECHANICS

In addition to the general requirements, an applicant
a. must not be more than 35 years by 1/11/2025 and must possess any of the following:
b. GES (COTVET) Certificate I or Certificate II or National Proficiency I or II Certificates in Automobile Engineering;
c. GES Intermediate Certificate in Vehicle Mechanic;
d. City & Guilds Certificate in Mechanical Engineering;
e. City & Guilds in Intermediate (Internal Combustion Engines and Motor Vehicle);
f. NVTI Proficiency Certificate I or Certificate II.

ii. DRIVERS/RIDERS

In addition to the general requirements an applicant;
a. must not be more than 35 years by 1/11/2025.
b. must either possess five (5) passes in SSSCE/WASSCE or

GES (COTVET) Certificate I or Certificate II or National Proficiency Certificates in any Technical or Vocational Programme or

City and Guilds Certificate in any Technical or Vocational Programme or

NVTI Proficiency Certificates in any Technical or Vocational Programme.
c. must possess a valid Driver’s License ‘B’ or above which should not be less than 3 years old;
d. must possess a valid Rider’s License ‘A’ which should not be less than 3 years old.

iii. ELECTRICIANS

In addition to the general requirements an applicant;
a. must not be more than 35 years by 1/11/2025.
b. Must possess either GES (COTVET) Certificate I or Certificate II or National Proficiency I or II Certificates in Electrical Installation in any Technical or Vocational Programme or
i. GES Intermediate Certificate in Electrical Installation in any Technical or Vocational Programme or
ii. City and Guilds Certificate in Electrical Engineering in any Technical or Vocational Programme or
iii. Electrical Engineering Technology Part I (GES) in any Technical or Vocational Programme or
iv. NVTI Proficiency II or Certificate I or Certificate II in any Technical or Vocational Programme.

iv. PLUMBERS & MASONS

In addition to the general requirements an applicant;
a. must not be more than 35 years by 1/11/2025.
b. Must possess either GES (COTVET) Certificate I or Certificate II or National Proficiency I or II Certificates in Plumbing and Masonry or
i. GES Intermediate Certificate in Plumbing/Masonry in any Technical or Vocational Programme or
ii. City and Guilds Certificate in Plumbing/Masonry in any Technical or Vocational Programme or
iii. NVTI Proficiency II or Certificate I or Certificate II in Plumbing/Masonry in any Technical or Vocational Programmes.

v. PAINTING/SIGN WRITING

In addition to the general requirements an applicant;
a. must not be more than 35 years by 1/11/2025.
b. Must possess either GES (COTVET) Certificate I or Certificate II or National Proficiency I or II Certificates in Painting/Sign Writing or
i. GES Intermediate Certificate in Painting/Sign Writing in any Technical or Vocational Programme or
ii. City and Guilds Certificate in Painting/Sign Writing in any Technical or Vocational Programme or
iii. NVTI Proficiency I or Certificate I or Certificate II in Painting/Sign Writing in any Technical or Vocational Programme.

vi. TAILORS/SEAMSTRESS

i. GES Intermediate Certificate in Tailoring in any Technical or Vocational Programme or
ii. NVTI Proficiency I or Certificate I or Certificate II in Tailoring in any Technical or Vocational Programme.

vii. WOOD CONSTRUCTION TECHNOLOGY (Carpentry)

i. GES Intermediate Certificate in Refrigeration in any Technical or Vocational Programme.

x. SPORTSMEN:
In addition to the general requirements an applicant;
a. must not be less than 18 years of age and not more than 30 years by 1/11/2025.
b. proven track record in recognized sports discipline.
c. be proficient in any of the following field:
i. Boxing
ii. Football
iii. Handball
iv. Basketball
v. Hockey
vi. Tennis
vii. Table tennis
viii. Volleyball
ix. Athletics (Track & Field)
x. Badminton
xi. Arm Wrestling
xii. Martial Art
d. Meet Ghana Police Service physical standards.
e. Pass full medical examination.
f. exhibit discipline, good character and team spirit.
g. pass sports specific trials during recruitment.
h. shortlisted players or professionals will undergo justification in their respective sports disciplines.
i. applicants affiliated with clubs and actively participate in recognized sports association leagues shall have an added advantage during the selection process.

xi. BAND:
In addition to the general requirements an applicant;
a. must not be more than 35 years by 1/11/2025.
b. must possess five (5) passes in SSSCE/WASSCE.
c. must have any of the following:
i. ability to sight-read musical notes.
ii. ability to play woodwind or brass instruments.
iii. Proficiency in basic music (ABRSM) Theory Grade 4 will be an added advantage.

C. GRADUATES (GENERAL DUTY):

I. Degree Holders:
In addition to the general requirements an applicant
i. must not be more than 35 years by 1/11/2025.
ii. must have a minimum of a Bachelor’s Degree (at least Second Class Lower Division) from any recognized university.

II. HND Holders:
In addition to the general requirements an applicant
i. must not be more than 32 years by 1/11/2025.
ii. must hold HND in any field of study from a recognized Technical University or any Tertiary Institution accredited by the Ghana Tertiary Education Commission (GTEC).

III. Diploma Holders:
In addition to the general requirements an applicant
i. must not be more than 32 years by 1/11/2025.
ii. must hold a Diploma in any field of study from a recognized Technical University or any Tertiary Institution accredited by the Ghana Tertiary Education Commission (GTEC).

D. MEDICAL PROFESSIONALS

i. MEDICAL DOCTORS:
In addition to the general requirements, an applicant

must not be more than 35 years by 1/11/2025.

must have MB ChB/BDS or its equivalent in addition to a permanent registration with the Ghana Medical and Dental Council.

must have at least one (1) year post-qualification working experience in a recognized health facility.

ii. MEDICAL SPECIALIST:
An applicant must not be more than 40 years by 1/11/2025.
Must be a member of the Medical and Dental Council of Ghana;

must have MB ChB/BDS or its equivalent from a recognized College;

plus, a relevant Post Graduate qualification to the level of Membership/Fellowship of the Ghana College of Physicians and Surgeons (GCPS/WACS/WACP) in addition to at least two (2) years post qualification experience in any of the following professions:
a. General Surgeons
b. Obstetrics / Gynecologists
c. Physician Specialists
d. Ophthalmology
e. Ear Nose and Throat
f. Doctor Anesthetist
g. Orthopedic Surgeon
h. Psychiatrist
i. Plastic Surgeon
j. Dental Surgeon
k. Emergency Physician Specialists
l. Radiologist
m. Pediatrician

iii. PHARMACIST:
In addition to the general requirements, an applicant;

must not be more than 35 years by 1/11/2025.

must possess a minimum of Bachelor of Pharmacy Degree (BPharm) (at least Second-class Lower Division) or Doctor of Pharmacy (PharmD) or Master in Pharmacy (MPharm) from a university accredited by the Ghana Accreditation Board.

be registered with the Pharmacy Council and the Pharmaceutical Society of Ghana with at least one (1) year post National Service working experience as a pharmacist.

Must be a member of the Pharmaceutical Society of Ghana.

iv. NURSING SERVICES:

must not be more than 32 years for HND/Certificate holders and 35 years for degree holders by 1/11/2025.

must be registered with the Nursing Midwifery Council.

j. Registered General Nurses
k. Certificate Nurses
l. Community Health Nurses
m. Nurse Assistants Clinical
n. Nurse Assistants Preventive

Pentecostal Council of Ghana

Office of the National/Regional Chief Imam
